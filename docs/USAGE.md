### Starting Playback

- **Click anywhere** on the video to play
- **Double-click** to enter fullscreen

### Pausing & Resuming

- **Click video** or press **Space** to pause
- **Click again** to resume from where you paused
- **Progress saved** automatically every 10 seconds

### Playback Speed

**Available speeds**: 0.75x, 1x, **1.25x**, 1.5x, 2x

- **0.75x** = Slower (better for studying dialogue)
- **1x** = Normal (default)
- **1.25x** = Slightly faster
- **1.5x** = Faster (popular for rewatching)
- **2x** = Double speed

**To change:**
1. Click **âš™ï¸ Settings**
2. Click **Playback Speed**
3. Select desired speed
4. Menu auto-closes

### Audio Tracks

Most episodes have multiple audio options:
- **Default** - First/preferred audio
- **English** - English dub (if available)
- **Japanese** - Original language (if available)
- **Other languages** - Depending on content

**To switch audio:**
1. Click **âš™ï¸ Settings**
2. Click **Audio Track**
3. Select desired language
4. Episode may briefly reload with new audio
5. Playback resumes from current position

**Note**: Switching audio may take a few seconds as the backend prepares the stream.

### Subtitles

**Available options:**
- **Off** - No subtitles
- **English** - English subtitles
- **Japanese** - Japanese subtitles
- **Romanized** - If available
- **Other languages** - Depending on content

**To enable subtitles:**
1. Click **âš™ï¸ Settings**
2. Click **Subtitles**
3. Select language
4. Subtitles appear at bottom of video
5. Menu closes

**Subtitle rendering:**
- White text on semi-transparent black background
- Auto-sized for readability
- Renders at bottom of video area

**Troubleshooting subtitles:**
- Try toggling **Off** then back to language
- Refresh episode if not loading
- Check subtitle file format (VTT preferred)

### External Player

Use MPV or VLC instead of built-in player:

**To open in external player:**
1. Click **ðŸ“¤ Pop-out** button
2. Video URL opens in default player (if configured)
3. Playback pauses in Animind (use external player now)

**Setup external player:**
1. Go to main **Settings** page (header navigation)
2. Set **MPV Path** or player executable path
3. Restart app
4. Next pop-out uses that player

### Efficient Workflow

1. **Resume from last watched:**
   - App remembers your position
   - Click episode to continue automatically

2. **Binge watching:**
   - Set speed to 1.25x or 1.5x for comfort
   - Use fullscreen mode to minimize distractions
   - Use keyboard shortcuts to keep hands free

3. **Study mode:**
   - Enable English subtitles
   - Set speed to 0.75x - 1x
   - Use pause/forward to rewatch dialogue

4. **Night mode:**
   - Dark theme enabled by default âœ“
   - Reduce brightness in OS settings if needed
   - Use mute during pauses to save audio

### Common Workflows

**Catching up on a series:**
1. Click series card from library
2. Click "Watch Now"
3. Click first unwatched episode
4. Use â†’ Arrow key to skip intro (usually 10-20 seconds)
5. After episode ends â†’ Next episode appears

**Rewatching a favorite:**
1. Click series card
2. Click "Watch Now"
3. Episodes show with resume positions
4. Click desired episode
5. Progress saved separately for each viewing

**Switching between series:**
1. Click back to library (header nav)
2. Select different anime
3. Click "Watch Now"
4. Your last watched episode per series is remembered
